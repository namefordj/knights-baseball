About The Knights

The Knights compete in the Pacific Coast Collegiate League (PCCL), which may be the largest summer collegiate league in California. This is a WOOD BAT league designed for highly competitive collegiate athletes.

Season Information

  • Season Dates: June 10 – July 22
  • Format: 24-game summer season
  • All games are Double Headers – two 7-inning games each meeting
  • 5 hours scheduled per doubleheader
  • 4 games per week over a 5‑week regular season
  • Playoffs take place during week 6
  • Each team has a mid‑week DH and a weekend DH

Additional Details

  • Practices: None
  • Out‑of‑state Travel: None

Player Registration

There is no fee to submit the player registration form. After reviewing your information, we will send an official invite, which you may accept or decline. A $200 non‑refundable deposit is required to secure your roster spot.

Player Fees

  • Position Player or Two‑Way: $700
  • Pitcher Only: $300

Fees cover all league game costs. Housing or travel is not included. All fees are due before June 1, 2026.

Uniforms

  • Jersey top and cap provided
  • Players must supply: white pants (any style), black socks, black belt
