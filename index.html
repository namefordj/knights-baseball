Info / About

Our League might be the biggest league in CA! This is a WOOD bat league.

Season Dates: June 10 – July 22

24-game summer season

  • All games are Double Headers
  • Two 7-inning games each matchup
  • 5 hours reserved per DH
  • 4 games per week – 5-week regular season
  • Playoffs take place week 6
  • One mid-week DH & one weekend DH per week

Practices: No

Out-of-state travel: No

There is no fee required to submit the player registration form. Once submitted, we review your info and send an official invite.

Deposit: $200 (nonrefundable) to secure your spot

Player Fees

  • Position or Two-way Player: $700
  • Pitcher Only: $300

Your player fee covers all game fees (no housing or travel included). All fees due by June 1, 2026.

Uniform

  • Jerseys + Cap provided
  • Players wear white pants, black socks, and black belt
